Accessibility Statement

Publications Division is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities.

Our Commitment

Publications Division is committed to making its website accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We strive to ensure that our website complies with accessibility standards and guidelines to provide an inclusive experience for everyone.

Accessibility Standards

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. Our website is designed to be:

  • Perceivable: Information and user interface components are presentable in ways users can perceive
  • Operable: User interface components and navigation are operable
  • Understandable: Information and the operation of user interface are understandable
  • Robust: Content is robust enough to be interpreted by a wide variety of user agents, including assistive technologies

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Alternative text for images and graphics
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Clear heading structure and page organization
  • Sufficient color contrast for text readability
  • Resizable text options
  • Descriptive link text
  • Form labels and error messages

Ongoing Efforts

We continuously work to improve the accessibility of our website. This includes:

  • Regular accessibility audits and testing
  • Training staff on accessibility best practices
  • Incorporating user feedback
  • Updating content and features to meet accessibility standards
