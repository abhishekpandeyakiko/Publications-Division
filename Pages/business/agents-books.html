Agents (Books)

Become an authorized agent for Publications Division books.

Join our network of authorized agents and help distribute quality publications across India. As an agent, you'll have access to our extensive catalog of books and benefit from attractive commission structures.

Benefits of Becoming an Agent

  • Access to comprehensive catalog of books
  • Attractive commission and discount structures
  • Marketing support and promotional materials
  • Priority access to new releases
  • Training and support from our team

Agent Requirements

To become an authorized agent for Publications Division books, you must:

  • Have a registered business entity
  • Maintain a physical or online retail presence
  • Meet minimum order requirements
  • Comply with our business policies and guidelines
  • Sign the standard agent agreement

How to Apply

Interested parties can apply to become an agent by:

  1. Downloading and reviewing the agent agreement
  2. Submitting the completed application form
  3. Providing necessary business documentation
  4. Awaiting approval from our business division
