Agents (Emp News)

Become an authorized agent for Employment News journal.

Employment News is the flagship weekly job journal launched in 1976 by the Ministry of Information & Broadcasting. Published in English, Hindi, and Urdu, it provides job vacancies, training programs, recruitment results, and career guidance for India's youth.

About Employment News

Employment News serves as a vital platform for employment opportunities across:

  • Central and State Governments
  • Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs)
  • Banks and Financial Institutions
  • Educational Institutions
  • Other Government Organizations

Benefits of Becoming an Employment News Agent

  • Weekly supply of high-demand publication
  • Attractive commission structure
  • Guaranteed demand from job seekers
  • Marketing support and promotional materials
  • Priority allocation for high-demand areas

Agent Requirements

To become an authorized Employment News agent, you must:

  • Have a registered business entity
  • Maintain a retail outlet or distribution network
  • Meet minimum weekly order requirements
  • Comply with our business policies and guidelines
  • Sign the standard Employment News agent agreement

Distribution Network

Employment News has a wide distribution network across India. As an agent, you'll be part of this network helping to reach job seekers in your area with timely information about employment opportunities.

How to Apply

Interested parties can apply to become an Employment News agent by:

  1. Reviewing the Employment News agent agreement
  2. Submitting the completed application form
  3. Providing necessary business documentation
  4. Awaiting approval from our business division
