Agents (Journals)

Become an authorized agent for Publications Division journals.

Join our network of authorized agents to distribute our monthly journals including Yojana, Kurukshetra, Bal Bharati, Aajkal, and Employment News across India.

Available Journals

  • Yojana: Published in 13 languages (English, Hindi, and 11 regional languages)
  • Kurukshetra: English and Hindi editions focusing on rural development
  • Bal Bharati: Popular Hindi children's monthly
  • Aajkal: Literary magazine in Hindi and Urdu
  • Employment News: Weekly job journal in English, Hindi, and Urdu
  • Other Regional Journals: Various language editions of our publications

Benefits of Becoming a Journal Agent

  • Regular monthly/weekly supply of journals
  • Attractive commission rates
  • Priority access to high-demand publications
  • Marketing support and promotional materials
  • Flexible subscription management

Agent Requirements

To become an authorized journal agent, you must:

  • Have a registered business entity
  • Maintain a retail outlet or distribution network
  • Meet minimum subscription requirements
  • Comply with our business policies and guidelines
  • Sign the standard journal agent agreement

How to Apply

Interested parties can apply to become a journal agent by:

  1. Reviewing the journal agent agreement terms
  2. Submitting the completed application form
  3. Providing necessary business documentation
  4. Awaiting approval from our business division
