The Salient Features of Home Library Scheme

Concession to Member

The Division will provide a discount of 20% on the books purchased by the members of the scheme. The minimum order under the scheme will be Rs.100/- after discount.

Free Postage

The postage on dispatch on the publications will be borne by the Publications Division i.e. Free of cost.

Catalogue of Books

The members will receive a Catalogue of Books free of cost.

Bonus Books

The members who will purchase the books exceeding Rs.500/- after discount during one financial year will get books/journals published by the Division worth Rs.25/- free of cost.

Operational Area

The scheme will be operational in Geographical boundaries of India only on pilot basis.
