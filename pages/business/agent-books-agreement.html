APPLICATION FORM FOR AGENTS OF BOOKS
OF PUBLICATIONS DIVISION
Agreement for Supply of DPD Books to an Authorized Agent

This Agreement is made on this   day of   20__ BETWEEN Publications Division, Government of India, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting having its office at “Soochna Bhawan”, CGO Complex, Lodhi Road, New Delhi – 110 003 acting through its DG/ADG/Director/Business Manager/Assistant Business Manager (HQ) for and on behalf of the “President of India” (hereinafter referred to as the Party of the First Part) AND M/s.   having their registered office at   and acting through its   (hereinafter called the Party of the Second Part).

WHEREAS the Party of the First Part is a Government Department under the Ministry of Information & Broadcasting and is engaged in the work of bringing out publications, sale, marketing and promotions of books & journals.

WHEREAS the Party of the Second Part is engaged in the sale of books / journals and has desired to sell, market and distribute the books / journals published by the Party of the First Part.

NOW THIS AGREEMENT WITNESSETH AS UNDER:

  1. The Agent shall deposit with the Publications Division (DPD) a sum of Rs. 1000 (Rupees One Thousand only) as a one-time non-refundable Registration Fee which will remain with the Publications Division as interest free security.
  2. This Dealership/Agency shall be non-transferable. Any further sale on credit shall be entirely at the Agent’s own risk.
  3. The Agent shall get discount on the printed price of the DPD Publications as per existing Business Policy:
    Sl. No. Monetary Limit Approved Discount
    1 Up to Rs. 10,000/- 25%
    2 Rs. 10,001 to Rs. 2 Lakh 35%
    3 Rs. 2,00,001 to Rs. 5 Lakh 40%
    4 Above Rs. 5 Lakh 45%
  4. Agents will be given 120 days credit facility against an EMD of 50% of gross purchase as per Business Policy.
  5. Agents availing 45% discount will be given a return facility of 20% of stock per year, except Annual Reference Book “India/Bharat”.
  6. The Agent shall sell publications at printed price, with reasonable discount allowed for libraries and institutions.
  7. Dispatch cost shall be borne as per Clause 4 of the Business Policy.
  8. Supplies shall be made on outright sale basis or approved credit facility.
  9. Agreement may be terminated by either party with one month notice.
  10. All disputes shall be subject to Delhi Courts only.
  11. Publications Division reserves the right to modify terms at its discretion.

(i) Party of the First Part
(For & on behalf of the President of India)

Signature & Seal

(ii) Party of the Second Part
