Clause 3 : Price of Digital Publications & Marketing Tie-Ups

(i) For e-Books, e-Journals & e-Aggregators

  • E-books available through multiple platforms including Publications Division website.
  • Price to be at par with print books with 25% discount (final price = 75% of P-Book).
  • Revenue sharing ratio: 70:30 (PD : Platform) on realised amount.
  • Special festival discount upto 20% for max 10 days during National Holidays & events.
  • Sale in Domestic & International market through e-commerce platforms.
  • E-resource aggregator may approach institutes/libraries (same terms as e-commerce platforms).
  • Same discount structure formula applicable as for P-Book (Clause 4).

(ii) Sale of P-Books on E-Commerce Platforms

  • Maximum discount ceiling up to 55%, including logistic costs of e-tailers.
  • Non-exclusive arrangement, cost to be covered under 55% discount limit.
  • Sale return allowed if order is cancelled / not honoured.
  • Agent manages stocking, selling, dispatch, promotion & grievances within 55% cost.
  • 120 days credit facility against 50% EMD of purchase value.
  • Return facility up to 20% of total purchased stock.
  • Agents must upload all PD titles on e-commerce platforms.

Clause 5 : Additional Discount for Old / Damaged Publications

  • Older than 5 years – 50% discount
  • Older than 10 years – 60% discount
  • Older than 15 years – 90% discount or eligible for donation to National Libraries & Govt Institutions
  • Last 10 copies retained in store as record copies.
  • Annual India/Bharat – Additional 35% discount in last 2 months of year.
  • Unsold journals after 1 year can be distributed free in events as sales promotion.

Writing Off Losses: As per GFR provisions.

Clause 6 : Credit & Exchange Facility for Agents

  • Agents will be given 120 days credit facility against 50% EMD. Credit can be increased against FD.
  • Govt departments & PSUs may get 60 days credit without EMD on written approval by Under Secretary or above.
  • 20% return facility on lifted stock allowed once a year for agents availing minimum 45% discount.
  • Returns not allowed for India/Bharat reference annual book due to short shelf life.

Clause 7 : Arrangement for Marketing of PD Publications

  • PD will collaborate with national publishing bodies such as NBT, Sahitya Academy and State Academies.
  • Partnerships to display & sell PD publications via institutions like IGNCA, Prasar Bharati, Film Division, FTII.

Clause 10 :

Special provisions for Strengthening Sales and Marketing :

(i) Engagement of five Zonal Marketing and Distribution Agencies (ZMDAs) for Journals and Books :

To improve the circulation of its journals, PD will engage five Zonal Marketing and Distribution Agencies (ZMDAs) for undertaking Sales and Marketing of its books/journals in order to ensure that its publications are easily available across to meet the unmet needs of readers. The five zones to market and distribute journals are Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Kolkata and Guwahati. The agencies to be engaged on zonal basis will also undertake aggressive sales and marketing efforts for improving the circulation of the books/journals and will facilitate greater visibility of PD’s publications across the country. The ZMDAs will be identified on the basis of an open advertisement.(Detailed Terms and Conditions to be separately finalised by PD in consultation with the Ministry)

(ii) Engagement of State level Empanelled Book Distribution Agencies (SLEBDs) for Promotion, Marketing and Sale of Books.

Presently, Publications Division’s sales & marketing efforts are undertaken through network of its book agents as well as its limited Sales Emporia. In order to widen its reach, PD envisages strengthening of its sales and marketing efforts by engaging State level Empanelled Book Distribution Agencies (SLEBDs) in all the States/UTs. Accordingly, PD envisages SLEBDs to act as an extended arm of PD that will undertake aggressive sales and marketing efforts to ensure greater reach for the books of Publications Division. The SLEBDs will also undertake participation and organisation of Book Fairs/Exhibitions on behalf of PD in their respective areas. The SLEBDs will be identified on the basis of an open advertisement. (Detailed Terms and Conditions to be separately finalised by PD in consultation with the Ministry)
