Royalty Structure

Information about the royalty structure for authors and contributors.

Royalty Structure & Copyright

Royalty payment to the authors is made at the rate of 15% of the print price of the book.

Authors may choose to retain the copyright of the book or assign it to the Publications Division.

If Copyright is given to Publications Division

A one-time lump-sum advance royalty is paid, calculated for 75% of copies of the first print order. No further royalty is paid thereafter.

If Copyright is retained by the Author

Advance royalty is paid for 50% of copies of the first print order. After adjustment, further royalty is paid at 15% of the sale price.
