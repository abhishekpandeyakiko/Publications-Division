Citizen Charter

Publications Division

Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

Our Mission

To give authentic and up-to-date information to the general public in India and abroad on topics of national importance. This is done by:

  • Publishing books and periodicals on Economy, History, Art & Culture, Heritage, Gandhian literature, biographies of national leaders and children’s literature.
  • Publishing selected speeches of Presidents and Prime Ministers of India.
  • Publishing Employment News weekly in Hindi, English and Urdu.
  • Publishing journals such as Yojana, Aajkal, Bal Bharti and Kurukshetra.
  • Ensuring wider reach through book exhibitions, events and integrated marketing.
  • Intent to enter the field of e-books.

Our Commitments to Our Clients

  • Book Buyers: Quality books at affordable prices.
  • Subscribers: Subscription processing within four weeks.
  • Authors: Publication within 8–10 months and timely royalty payment.
  • Distributors: Timely supply of books and journals.

Expectations from Our Readers

  • Prompt feedback on content quality and presentation.
  • Suggestions for new titles and improvements.
  • Authors may submit proposals aligned with Publications Division objectives.
